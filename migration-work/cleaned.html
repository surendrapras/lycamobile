FAQs

Is roaming included with my pay monthly UK SIM only plan?

Yes. Lyca Mobile offers inclusive EU roaming with many of its pay monthly bundles, letting you use your minutes, texts, and data across Europe at no extra cost. This means if you're heading to France, Italy or Germany, you can roam like at home.  

 

Our customers on pay monthly contracts benefit from seamless access to roaming services. For travel outside the EU, additional roaming data bundles may be needed to avoid unexpected charges.  

 

For a full breakdown of countries, view our Countries by Zone section.  
 

Want to learn how to enable roaming? Check out how to activate EU and international roaming .  

How do I activate my pay monthly eSIM?

Once you’ve selected an eSIM-compatible pay monthly plan during checkout, we’ll send a QR code to your email. You simply scan the code via your phone’s mobile settings to activate your plan .  

 

Whether you’re using the latest iPhone, a Samsung or Pixel device, our eSIM setup ensures a sustainable, dual SIM-ready experience. You don’t need to wait for a physical SIM delivery! This is perfect for travellers and digital-first users.  

 

Visit our eSIM support hub to view device-specific setup steps.  

Is there a credit check for pay monthly SIM only contracts?

Yes, Lyca Mobile performs a credit check for pay monthly UK SIM only deals as part of the onboarding process. This helps ensure responsible account use and allows us to offer competitive monthly prices with large data allowances.  

 

If you'd prefer a no-commitment plan, explore our Pay As You Go SIM cards , which don’t require any financial verification or long-term contract.  

 

Unsure which is right for you? Compare our pay monthly and PAYG plans to find the best fit.  

How do I transfer my number to a new pay monthly SIM only plan?

If you’re moving to a new SIM only plan and want to keep your number, it’s as easy as:  

  1. Getting a PAC code from your current provider.  

  1. Activating your new Lyca Mobile SIM plan.  

  1. Submitting your PAC via My Lyca or during activation.  

We usually complete number transfers within one working day, ensuring minimal disruption to your service.  

 

Full guide: How to transfer or port your number  

What happens if I don’t pay my pay monthly SIM contract on time?

If your pay monthly bill isn’t paid on time, Lyca Mobile may temporarily suspend services including calls, texts, and data. You'll be notified with reminders and provided options to settle your balance via My Lyca or the Lyca Mobile app.  

 

Continued non-payment can result in:  

  • Late fees  

  • Service disconnection  

  • Damage to your credit rating  

  • Termination of contract  

To prevent these issues, we recommend setting up Direct Debit payments and keeping track of your billing cycle using our Billing & Payment Support resources.  

 

Facing issues? Contact Lyca Mobile pay monthly Support
