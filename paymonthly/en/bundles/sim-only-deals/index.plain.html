FAQs

Is roaming included with my pay monthly UK SIM only plan?
Yes, roaming is included with all our pay monthly SIM only plans. You can use your allowances when travelling within the EU at no extra cost.
How do I activate my pay monthly eSIM?
To activate your pay monthly eSIM, you'll need to download the Lyca Mobile app and follow the simple setup instructions. You'll receive a QR code to scan, which will activate your eSIM instantly.
Is there a credit check for pay monthly SIM only contracts?
Yes, a credit check is required for all pay monthly SIM only contracts to ensure responsible borrowing and help us provide you with the best service.
How do I transfer my number to a new pay monthly SIM only plan?
To transfer your number, simply request a PAC code from your current provider and provide it to us during the sign-up process. Your number will be transferred within one working day.
What happens if I don't pay my pay monthly SIM contract on time?
If you miss a payment, your service may be temporarily suspended. We'll contact you to arrange payment and restore your service as quickly as possible.
